Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, especially for weekends and bridal services. Walk-ins are welcome subject to availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you offer group bookings?

Absolutely! We offer special packages for bridal parties, birthdays, and group events. Contact us for customized quotes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment apps. Payment is due at the time of service.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes! We prioritize using cruelty-free and vegan products whenever possible. We can accommodate specific product preferences.

Do you offer trial sessions for bridal makeup?

Yes, all our bridal packages include at least one trial session. We recommend scheduling it 1-2 months before your wedding.
